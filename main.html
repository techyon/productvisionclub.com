Our Vision

Our vision is to build the next generation of student leaders to tackle the world's challenges, create innovative solutions and make positive impact using technology.

Our Mission

Foster Leadership

Gain Product Perspective

Develop an Entrepreneurial Mindset

We want to foster leadership, stimulate product perspective and inspire big thinking, entrepreneurial mindsets through interactions with some of the biggest players and thought leaders in the industry by hosting workshops, talks and events for our community of visionaries.

How We Started

The University of Waterloo has great infrastructure for churning out top technical talent, however there is a gap when it comes to looking at technology from a higher perspective - in terms of societal and personal impact. As budding PMs, entrepreneurs and technology enthusiasts, we wanted to explore the rationale behind what we should be build and why. We wanted to figure out how to change the world, learn from the best in the process build a community of talented and passionate students who share our vision.

What We Do

Tech talks

Hear about how it’s like to work as a PM/engineer in the industry. Interactive Q&A with our past speakers from top companies and successful start-ups

Why
  • To hear about how it's like to work as a PM in the industry
  • To learn about speaker's past experiences and their journeys
  • To receive general career advice
  • To gain product perspectives and participate in interactive Q&A sessions
Educational Sessions

A chance to participate in sessions which are led by interns and full-time incoming PMs from Microsoft, Groupon, etc

Why
  • To hear about product management (PM) internship experiences and lessons
  • To learn how to prepare for PM interviews
  • To learn how to land PM co-op and full-time placements

OnePM

The product-focused hackathon to determine the one true PM!

Why
  • Product-minded hackathon to learn how great products are built skills
  • Get a chance to apply your skills to take your ideas and prototype new products 
  • Gain valuable experience to prepare for interviews and future PM placements

Innovation Trek

Exclusive trek program that brings a select group of UWaterloo students to visit and interact with leadership from world leading technology companies

Why
  • Interact with management and thought leaders from top technology companies
  • Exclusive networking opportunity to learn from people in industry
  • Join our fellows and alumni network for career opportunities and advice

Hacker House

Starting Fall 2015 in Seattle, Washington!

  • Receive personal mentorship from top UW interns and alum, a space to live & work in style, and access to exclusive house hack days and talks!
  • Fully furnished with luxury amenities, convenient commuting options to Bellevue or Redmond
  • Valuable community and networking experience, cheaper than most corporate housing options (by $200+/month)
