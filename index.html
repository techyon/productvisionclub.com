Our Vision

Our vision is to build the next generation of student leaders to tackle the world's challenges, create innovative solutions and make positive impact using technology.

Our Mission

Foster Leadership

Gain Product Perspective

Develop an Entrepreneurial Mindset

Our mission is to foster product perspective, develop entrepreneurial mindsets and inspire big thinking through interactions with PMs, executives, founders and thought leaders from industry by hosting workshops, talks and events for our community of visionaries.

How We Started

The University of Waterloo has great infrastructure for churning out top technical talent, however there is a gap in the community when it comes to looking at technology from a higher perspective - in terms of societal and personal impact. As budding PMs, entrepreneurs and technology enthusiasts, we wanted to explore the rationale behind what we should be build and why. We wanted to figure out how to change the world, learn from the best in the process build a community of talented and passionate students who share our vision.

What We Do

Tech talks

Hear about how it’s like to work as a PM/engineer in the industry. Interactive Q&A with our past speakers from top companies and successful start-ups

Why
  • To hear about how it's like to work as a PM in the industry
  • To learn about speaker's past experiences and their journeys
  • To receive general career advice
  • To gain product perspectives and participate in interactive Q&A sessions
Educational Sessions

A chance to participate in sessions which are led by interns and full-time incoming PMs from Microsoft, Groupon, etc

Why
  • To hear about product management (PM) internship experiences and lessons
  • To learn how to prepare for PM interviews
  • To learn how to land PM co-op and full-time placements

Product Vision Challenge

Hear about product management (PM) internship experiences and lessons and learn how to prepare for interviews to land PM co-op or full-time placements

Why
  • To step into the shoes of a PM
  • To get a chance to apply your skills
  • To gain valuable experience
  • To develop a more entrepreneurial mindset

Innovation Trek

Exclusive trek program that brings a select group of UWaterloo students to visit and interact with leadership from world leading technology companies

Why
  • To have a chance at networking exclusively
  • To connect with other like-minded top students with diverse talents
  • To join our fellows and alumni network for career opportunities and advice
  • To interact with leaders from top technology companies
