Our Vision

Our vision is to build the next generation of student leaders to tackle the world's challenges, create innovative solutions and make positive impact using technology.

Our Mission

Foster Leadership

Gain Product Perspective

Develop an Entrepreneurial Mindset

We want to foster leadership, stimulate product perspective and inspire big thinking, entrepreneurial mindsets through interactions with some of the biggest players and thought leaders in the industry by hosting workshops, talks and events for our community of visionaries.

How We Started

The University of Waterloo has great infrastructure for churning out top technical talent, however there is a gap when it comes to looking at technology from a higher perspective - in terms of societal and personal impact. As budding PMs, entrepreneurs and technology enthusiasts, we wanted to explore the rationale behind what we should be build and why. We wanted to figure out how to change the world, learn from the best in the process build a community of talented and passionate students who share our vision.
